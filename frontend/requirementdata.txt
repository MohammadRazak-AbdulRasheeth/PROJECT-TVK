2. ABOUT US PAGE 
Who Are We 
TVK Canada is the official fan club for Thalapathy Vijay in Canada, created to unite 
fans, families, and Tamil Canadians through celebration, culture, and community. We 
are a nonprofit organization built on passion, togetherness, and the shared 
admiration for Vijay’s talent, discipline, and values. 
Our Mission 
Our mission is to bring people together through meaningful community building, 
memorable events, and a positive fan experience. We aim to create a welcoming 
space where supporters of Vijay can connect, celebrate, and enjoy being part of a 
vibrant Canadian fan community. 
Our Values 
We are guided by principles that reflect the spirit of our members: 
● Unity — forming strong bonds across cities and cultures 
● Democracy — encouraging openness, equality, and fairness within our 
community 
● Respect — treating everyone with kindness, dignity, and inclusiveness 
What We Do 
TVK Canada hosts exciting, family-friendly, and engaging activities throughout the 
year, including: 
● Movie Nights to enjoy Vijay’s films together 
● Watch Parties for major releases and special moments 
● Weekly Meetups to build friendships and community spirit 
● Cultural gatherings, celebrations, and fan events 
We are more than a fan club, we are a joyful and growing Canadian community 
brought together by our love and admiration for Thalapathy Vijay.

FAQ 
1. When does membership launch? 
Membership will officially open soon. Announcements will be posted on our website 
and social media. 
2. How do I get my membership card? 
Your physical membership card will be mailed to the address you provide. Delivery takes 
approximately 2–4 weeks. 
3. What is the Special Edition Founding Member Card? 
A limited-edition card with premium design, given only to the first 200 members. 
4. What discounts do members get? 
TVK partners with Canadian businesses to provide: 
● Event discounts 
● Food & product offers 
● Free & discounted movie nights 
● Exclusive promotions 
A full list of partner benefits will appear on the website. 
5. Do you offer family or group plans? 
Not at launch, but we plan to roll out family packages in the future. 
6. Can I cancel my membership? 
Yes. 
● Monthly memberships can be cancelled anytime. 
● Yearly memberships are final but include additional perks. 
7. Do I need my card for events? 
Yes. Your physical membership card must be shown to receive member pricing, 
discounts, and access. 
8. Are events only in Toronto? 
No. TVK Canada will host events all across Canada, including: 
Toronto, GTA, Ottawa, Montreal, Calgary, Edmonton, Winnipeg, Vancouver, Surrey, 
Halifax, and more. 
Our goal is to bring TVK events to every major Canadian city. 
9. How often are events held? 
We plan to host weekly events, plus major gatherings for movie releases and special 
celebrations. 
10. How will I receive event updates? 
Members will get priority email notifications, text alerts, and access to exclusive 
member communication channels.
